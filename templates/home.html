Beta Trials


01

How it Works

Intake Call

Meet with us for a quick 30-45 min. call, during which,we will get to know you better. We'll have a very relaxed conversation about your background, expereience, and what you're looking for in your next role

02

Resume Updates & Job Search


We update your resume for you, and conduct a preliminary search of best-fit open roles according to the intake interview. At the end of this step, you'll have a professionally updated resume, and a list of open jobs, for which you are a strong candidate.

03

Application


Using the updated resume and list of job posts provided, you apply to the jobs. We don't currently provide cover letters, so if any of the applications requires one, you'll need to write one for that application.

04

Record Results


We will provide a simply spreadsheet to track applications and their outcome. The goal is to get called back for an initial interview.

05

Conclusion


Step 5 above is the last phase of our Beta Trials, but our involvement doesn't stop there. As part of our high-touch placement services, we also offer interview prep, salary negotiation advice, and more!
